Sadza

Description

This is a traditional African meal prepared for centuries on the Continent This dish can be prepared for morning, lunch and dinner eating

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Mealie Meal
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Add a pinch of salt
  3. Stir in mealie meal to boiling water
  4. Stir and mold until desired firmness
  5. Seamer for 15 mins
