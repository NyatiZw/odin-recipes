Mashed Potatoes

Description

This is a quick meal prepared for on the go meals This dish can be prepared for morning, lunch and dinner especially for the little ones

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Six (6) Potatoes
  • Salt
  • milk

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Add a pinch of salt
  3. Add in Potatoes to boiling water
  4. Wait until potatoes are soft and crumle upon touch
  5. Strain the water and mash them adding milk until desired consistency
