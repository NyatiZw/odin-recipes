Eland Steak with Apples

Description

This is a traditional African meal prepared for centuries on the Continent This dish is best for your family bbq

Ingredients

  • 0,75hg eland steak
  • 2 cooking apples
  • 20g brown sugar
  • 25g butter
  • olive oil
  • 25ml lime juice
  • a dash of brown rum
  • freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the apples
  2. Sprinkle apple slices with brown sugar
  3. then place in pan and gently soften with butter
  4. keep apples warm while steaks are seasoned and fried until pink
  5. Take steaks out and keep warm
  6. Cool pan with lime hjuice and rum
  7. This sauce is poured onto the plate
  8. The meat is arranged on top of it and covered with slices of apple
  9. Delicious Mashed Potatoes are served with this
